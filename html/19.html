Mette-Marit

First brewed: 06/2013

PH: 4.4

Attenuation level: 83

Boil volume: 25 litres

Food pairing:

  • Fårikål (mutton stew) with a spicy twist
  • Fenalår (salted cured lamb)
  • Krumkake with lingonberry jam

Date de dégustation:

We sent this beer to Norway where it was known as 'Hello, my name is Censored’. You can make up your own mind as to why. This brew was a red berry explosion, with a resinous bitter edge layered with dry berry tartness.

Brewers tips: Using crushed cranberries will dry out the beer further, drawing out their natural astringent character.

Contributed by: Sam Mason (samjbmason)

Malt
Extra Pale 6.12 kg
Caramalt 0.8 kg

Running the knife-edge between an India Pale Ale and a Stout, this particular style is one we truly love. Black IPAs are a great showcase for the skill of our brew team, balancing so many complex and twisting flavours in the same moment. The citrus, mango, and pine from the hops – three of our all-time favourites – play off against the roasty dryness from the malt bill at each and every turn.
